GCSE - General Certificate of Secondary Education

At SLA, we are currently running revision courses for those students who have prior GCSE knowledge and who wish to retake certain GCSEs to unlock their highest potential.

About the course

At SLA we provide GCSE and IGCSE retake courses and it is designed for those who have failed to achieve their potential or do not have strong enough GCSE grades to allow for a successful university and/or a life’s career. Often students underestimate the revision time needed or the importance of this qualification and underperform. This course is for those who cannot come to the UK as yet due to COVID-19 pandemic, feel safer staying at home or have vulnerable and at-high-risk family members at home that makes going to regular schools a difficult and risky choice. We provide a safe and highly interactive environment for students to learn and prepare for their future careers.

We provide made-to-measure courses for 16-20 year-olds who want to better their chances of a brighter future. There is considerable tutor input to ensure that students are fully supported and guided through their course. Extensive use of past exam questions/booklets and complete papers at appropriate times with full tutor input and advice are highly effective techniques in ensuring that students are well prepared for their final exams.

Although the college offers a particular specification for each subject for its full GCSE/IGCSE, our highly experienced tutors are experts at teaching all the other specifications, including, AQA, Edexcel, Eduqas (WJEC), International Cambridge and OCR.

SLA is the Home of Best Tutors all gathered under one ‘cyber roof’. Take advantage of it.
