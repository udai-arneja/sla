Meet Ms. Freeman

Ms. Freeman has spent the majority of her teaching career at an independent sixth form college in Central London, working in the Senior Management. She has successfully accelerated students from average to the highest grades, resulting in several students winning CIFE awards, at the House of Lords, for being the most improved pupils across a range of independent sixth form colleges.

Throughout her 20 years of teaching she has amassed a comprehensive range of knowledge in both Economics and Business. Her revision focus remains on time management, answering the specific questions and meeting the specifics of the mark scheme.

Ms. Freeman specialises in teaching Economics A Level for AQA, Edexcel, and OCR. She was an AQA examiner for over a decade and now her teaching strategy provides students with a comprehensive understanding of how to hit the mark schemes. She focuses on revision techniques, with exam-focused intensive tuition and her methods of learning involve helping students to collate a pack of essay plans in addition to their class notes. This gives students an understanding of how to utilise their school work in addition to tuition in an exam situation. She have a range of resources, all exam board specific and have made multiple choice papers, to ensure key terms are learnt, so that students write with the subject appropriate vocabulary
