The Principal's Welcome

Dear Visitor,

Welcome to SLA and thank you for spending your time finding about us.

I have been an academic all my life. Whether as a research scientist specialising on viruses in a past life, or a successful teacher of Biology and an administrator at present. Over the past 35 years I have learnt valuable lessons which I aim to pass onto the next generations. I have come to realise that a good strategy, a desire to succeed, a willingness to work hard and excellent advisors/tutors are all that one needs to achieve the best in any field.

I have expertise in UCAS applications and have managed to send many of my students onto medical, dental, physiotherapy, pharmacy, veterinary and optometry courses all of whom are successful professionals.

I have tried to provide an array of courses and subjects to help students in any walk of life and in any situation. The courses can be tailor-made to the needs of the student and this will come to light after a face-to-face interview.

I have tried to keep the website simple and to the point. If at any time you feel you would like to talk to me in person, please complete the form under ‘contact us’ and I will be in touch as soon as is possible. Alternatively, you could try contacting us by email or telephone.

If a course you need is not mentioned, discuss it with me. With my years of experience, I believe I may be in a position to help.

I wish you all a safe and virus-free day.

Warm regards,

Dr Pantea Etessami

Principal
